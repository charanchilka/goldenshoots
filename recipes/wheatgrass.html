1. Basic Wheatgrass Shot

Ingredients

  • 1 oz fresh wheatgrass (or about a handful)
  • 1 cup of water (for blending)
  • Small piece of ginger (Optional)
  • 1 tsp honey or jaggery powder (Optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Juice the wheatgrass using a juicer. If using a blender, add water and blend, then strain.
  2. Drink immediately as a shot for maximum nutrients.
  3. Consume within 15â€“30 minutes to preserve freshness and benefits.

2. Wheatgrass Lemon Detox

Ingredients

  • 1 oz wheatgrass juice
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup water or coconut water
  • 1 tsp honey, maple syrup, or jaggery (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a glass or shaker bottle.
  2. Stir or shake well. Serve chilled.

3. Green Power Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 oz wheatgrass
  • 1 green apple
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/2 lemon
  • Small piece of ginger

Instructions

  1. Juice all ingredients using a juicer.
  2. Stir and enjoy cold.

4. Wheatgrass Mango Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 oz wheatgrass juice
  • 1/2 cup mango chunks (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Serve immediately.

5. Tropical Wheatgrass Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 oz wheatgrass juice
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • Splash of lime juice

Instructions

  1. Mix all juices in a shaker or blender.
  2. Serve chilled over ice.

6. Banana Citrus Wheatgrass Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 oz wheatgrass juice
  • 1 banana
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • 1/2 cup water or almond milk
  • Ice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until creamy.
  2. Serve immediately for a nutrient-rich breakfast or snack.
